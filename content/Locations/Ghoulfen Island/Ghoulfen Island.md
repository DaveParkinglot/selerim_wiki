- Domain of [[Locations/Ghoulfen Island/Galgitch, The Afterking]]
- A curse left on the land by a long dead lich means that nothing that dies in Ghoulfen Island stays dead, unless the creature so chooses to forego the "opportunity" to live in death
- [[Locations/Ghoulfen Island/Mournholde]], the city of the dead, has become a bastion for those who would prefer to live in death
	- It is not uncommon for an elderly or sick person to attempt the journey to Mournholde, before their time is up...
	- For those who cannot afford the expensive journey to Mournholde, there is a chance to win free passage aboard the [[Locations/Ghoulfen Island/The Necronaut of Mournholde]] via a lottery system
